DOH Launches New Program about Teen Mental Health and Disaster Awareness

Published: October 21, 2025

the Department of Health (DOH) strengthened its Peer Support Groups Playbook Program, which was launched today among students of Cavite National High School. The program highlights the importance of strengthening peer support groups to increase young people’s awareness of mental health and to encourage mutual support among students during disasters or in facing everyday challenges.

DOH New Campaign: TRANGKASO BYE-BYE!

Published: October 20, 2025

The Department of Health (DOH) launched the “Trangkaso Bye-Bye!” campaign to educate the public on flu prevention through simple habits like handwashing, resting at home, and eating fruits and vegetables. Despite no flu outbreak, the DOH reminded the public that the country remains in flu season amid the changing monsoon. Recent data showed 6,457 influenza-like illness (ILI) cases from September 28 to October 11, 2025 — a 25% decrease compared to the same period in 2024.

⚠️Health Alerts & Advisories

🚨 Dengue Fever Advisory

There has been a recent increase in dengue cases in the city. Please use mosquito repellents and ensure there is no stagnant water around your home.

Date Issued: October 25, 2025

🌋 Ashfall and Lahar

Due to the sudden volcanic activity of Mount Kanlaon, the Department of Health (DOH) advises the public to take necessary precautions to protect their health and safety. People are urged to wear masks, preferably N95, or cover their nose and mouth with a damp cloth to avoid inhaling volcanic ash.

Date Issued: October 23, 2025

😷 Flu Season

The DOH clarified that there is no outbreak. However, since it is the usual flu season, the public is still advised to take precautions to avoid getting sick.
