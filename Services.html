MEET OUR ASSIGNED HEALTH WORKERS

Dr. Jonathan Dela Cruz

Dr. Dela Cruz is a dedicated family physician with over 12 years of experience in community medicine. He focuses on preventive healthcare and patient wellness education for all ages.

Ms. Salima Gonzales

Ms. Gonzales is a licensed midwife specializing in prenatal and maternal care. She has been serving mothers in Barangay Mabini for over 8 years with compassion and dedication.

Mr. John Michael Panganiban

Mr. Panganiban is a skilled public health nurse passionate about senior citizen wellness programs. He ensures proper monitoring of patients and promotes healthy living habits.

Ms. Michelle Macapagal

Ms. Macapagal is a community health worker who assists in vaccination drives and child health programs. Her approachable nature makes her a trusted member of the local clinic.
